Advancement System

Training · Magical Growth · Transformative Events

🏋️ Training (Time-based)

  • 1 month training: +1 to one skill check
  • 6 months training: Gain new skill proficiency
  • 1 year training: +1 to one attribute (max 20)

✨ Magical Growth

  • Glyph Mastery: +1 INK per 5 glyphs mastered
  • Animent Expansion: +1 max ANI per month of active casting
  • Outline Proficiency: Using outlines reduces their cost over time

🔥 Transformative Events

  • Glyph Bonding: Permanent ANI increase, but SAN cost
  • Elemental Convergence: Gain minor traits of secondary element
  • Rituals: Can alter biological stats (ATS/HTS) with risk

🎯 Adventuring Tier

Tier is based on experience, not levels. Used in Health calculation.

  • Tier 1: Novice – less than 1 year experience
  • Tier 2: Initiate – 1-3 years
  • Tier 3: Adept – 4-7 years
  • Tier 4: Veteran – 8-15 years
  • Tier 5: Master – 16+ years
