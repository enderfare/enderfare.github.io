RESOURCES

Mental & Energy Resources

🧠 Sanity (SAN)

Sanity = [Wisdom × 5] + [Intellect + Presence]
Current / Maximum format

Affected by: witnessing horrors, glyph backlash, Annihilation exposure.

Threshold Effect
75%+ Stable, normal function
50-74% Nightmares, -1 concentration
25-49% Hallucinations, disadvantage on mental saves
10-24% Psychosis, random glyph misfires
<10% Broken, loss of magical control

⚡ Energy (ENR)

Energy = [Endurance × 4] + [Might × 2]
Current / Maximum. Physical stamina.

Recovers with rest: 8 hours = full recovery.

✨ Animent (ANI)

Animent = [Insight × 3] + [Presence × 2] + Element Base
Magical energy pool for glyph activation.

Element Base:

  • Mythic: +30
  • Primordial: +25
  • Fundamental: +20
  • Physical: +15
  • Terrestrial: +10

Recovery: Meditation (1 hour = 10% recovery).

🖋️ Glyphic Ink (INK)

INK = Base 1 + [Intellect/4] + Affinity Bonus
Fixed pool. Maximum inscribed glyphs. Mageborn only.

Affinity Bonus:

  • Mythic: +5
  • Primordial: +4
  • Fundamental: +3
  • Physical: +2
  • Terrestrial: +1
