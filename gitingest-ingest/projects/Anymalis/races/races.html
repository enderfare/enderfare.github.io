Races of Anymalis

Three core lineages – Felinnari, Corveki, Drifters

🐆 Felinnari (Feline-folk)

Elite trackers, guardians, glyph-wardens. Respected but aloof. +2 ATS, claws, keen senses, free Guardian Outline.

→ Full Felinnari page

🪶 Corveki (Raven-folk)

Master crafters, knowledge-hoarders. Valued but secretive. +2 ATS, flight, free Connection Outline 1/day.

→ Full Corveki page

🧍 Drifters (Humans – "Clay-Folk")

Adaptable nomads, distrusted outsiders. ATS 0, two extra skill proficiencies, can learn secondary element.

⚙️ Creating Your Character

  1. Assign Attribute Scores: 72-point buy. Races may provide bonuses (e.g., Felinnari: +1 Finesse, +1 Insight).
  2. Note Special Resources: Glyphic Ink – Felinnari/Corveki get affinity bonus; Drifters start with base only.
  3. Record ATS: +2 for Felinnari/Corveki, 0 for Drifters.
  4. Select Proficiencies: Based on race and background. Drifters get two extra choices.
  5. Choose Element & Variant: Drifters can later study a second element.
