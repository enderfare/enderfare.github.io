CORE MECHANIC

Physical & Mental Attributes

Six attributes · 72‑point buy · D&D inspired

The Six Attributes

Might (STR)

Physical power, lifting, melee damage.

Finesse (DEX)

Agility, reflexes, precision.

Endurance (CON)

Health, resilience, stamina.

Intellect (INT)

Knowledge, reasoning, glyph comprehension.

Insight (WIS)

Perception, intuition, magical sensitivity.

Presence (CHA)

Willpower, persuasion, magical projection.

Stat Generation

72‑Point Buy
• Base 8 in all attributes
• 72 points to distribute
• Maximum 18 before racial modifiers
• Cost: 1 point per +1 (e.g., 8 → 12 costs 4 points)

Skills by Attribute

  • Might: Athletics, Intimidation (physical)
  • Finesse: Acrobatics, Sleight of Hand, Stealth
  • Intellect: Arcana (glyphs), History, Investigation, Lore
  • Insight: Animal Handling, Medicine, Perception, Survival
  • Presence: Deception, Performance, Persuasion, Leadership

Saving Throws

  • Physical: Might, Finesse, Endurance
  • Mental: Intellect, Insight, Presence

Characters start with 2 saving throw proficiencies based on background.

Proficiency System

No levels – proficiency improves through training:

  • Trained: +2 to skill checks
  • Expert: +4 to skill checks
  • Master: +6 to skill checks
