📜 Special Rules

Glyph Casting Costs

  • Simple Glyph: 1-3 ANI
  • Complex Glyph: 4-7 ANI
  • Ritual Glyph: 8+ ANI
  • Outlines: Add 1-2 ANI per outline stacked

Trait Interactions

  • Animal traits may provide natural weapons (1d4 damage)
  • Hybrid traits may provide darkvision or special senses
  • High Q% causes social stigma (-2 Presence in conservative societies)

Magical Birthmarks

  • Provide advantage on glyph saves of matching element
  • Glow when using affinity element (can't be hidden)
  • Can be used as focus for casting (no material components needed)
