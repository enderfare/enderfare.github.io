Welcome to Anymalis

Glyphic Resonance · v1.1.1

Anymalis is a TTRPG system built on the Glyphic Resonance engine – a world where magic is written, races carry animal souls, and every character is marked by an elemental birthmark.

🌍 The World

Anymalis is a land of ancient forests, floating aerie cities, and drifting caravans. Magic flows through glyphs – primal symbols that are combined with outlines to shape reality. Every mageborn person bears a birthmark that encodes their innate elemental affinity; the rarer the variant, the greater the power.

🎲 What Makes Anymalis Unique?

  • Level‑less advancement – grow through training, not XP.
  • Animal & hybrid traits – your lineage gives you claws, wings, or keen senses.
  • Quimera heritage – mixed‑species characters with unique mechanical depth.
  • Elemental hierarchy – 11 elements, from Terrestrial (10 variants) to Mythic (1 variant).
  • Sanity, Energy & Animent – three resource tracks that affect gameplay.

📜 Core Philosophy

“Fewer variants = more power.” A character born with the Light glyph (2 variants) will always be more magically potent than one born with Wind (10 variants), but the latter can achieve incredible subtlety. Player choice, not random chance, defines your story.

✨ Glyphic Ink (INK) = 1 + (Intellect/4) + Affinity Bonus
The number of glyphs you can inscribe – your magical vocabulary.
