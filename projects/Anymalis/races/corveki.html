PLAYABLE RACE

🪶 Corveki

Raven‑folk · master crafters · knowledge‑hoarders

Society & Role

Living in intricate aerial cities or complex roosts built into canyon walls, the Corveki are hoarders of knowledge and rare materials. Their society values cleverness, artistic expression, and long-term planning. They are master crafters, especially of glyphic ink and magical foci, but their trade is governed by riddles, favors, and complex contracts.

Physical Traits

They appear as slender humanoids with sleek, black feathers covering their arms, shoulders, and back, culminating in large, powerful wings. Their eyes are deep and intelligent, often seeming to hold secrets. Skilled fliers, they are known for sudden, silent landings.

Mechanical Integration

🧬 Animal Traits (ATS +2)
• Avian Wings: Fly speed equal to walking speed. Heavy armor interferes.
• Keen Mind: Proficient in Insight or Investigation (choose at creation).
🔗 Glyphic Affinity – Connection Outline
Once per long rest, when you inscribe a glyph, you can apply an additional Connection Outline for free. This allows you to link spell effects with uncanny precision.

Quimera & Heritage

Corveki heritage adds +5% to Quimera Percentage (Q%). Hybrids may have vestigial wings (gliding only) or iridescent feather patches.

Sample Names

Kaelen, Morwena, Corvin, Astrafel, Nera, Orin, Vesper.
