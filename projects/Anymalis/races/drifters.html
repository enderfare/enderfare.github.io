PLAYABLE RACE

🧍 Drifters (Humans)

"Clay-Folk" · adaptable nomads · distrusted outsiders

Society & Role

Viewed with suspicion for their "lack of a true nature," humans are perpetual wanderers. They have no ancestral homelands, living in caravans, ships, or temporary settlements. Other races see them as untrustworthy cultural scavengers, thieves of ideas, and dangerously unpredictable because they cannot be easily categorized. Despite this, their settlements often become vital trade crossroads where no other race would meet.

Physical Traits

Their most defining feature is their sheer diversity in appearance, making them hard to identify as a coherent group. Many other races find their "unfinished" visage unsettling.

Mechanical Integration

🧬 Adaptable Nature (ATS 0)
• No fixed animal traits
• Choose proficiency in any two skills or tools at character creation
• This does not increase ATS
✨ Glyphic Affinity (or Lack Thereof)
• Do NOT get the standard Glyphic Ink bonus from elemental affinity
• Base INK = 1 + (Intellect/4) only
• Compensation: Can attune to a secondary element after one month of dedicated study
• Once attuned, learn and cast glyphs from that element at reduced penalty

Quimera & Heritage

Drifters have +0% Q% modifier. Their offspring with other races always take the non-human parent's ATS baseline, diluted.

Sample Names

Elara, Theron, Mira, Kael, Sera, Doran, Lina, Corin.
