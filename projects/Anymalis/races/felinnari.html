PLAYABLE RACE

🐆 Felinnari

Feline‑folk · elite trackers · glyph‑wardens

Society & Role

The Felinnari are elite trackers, guardians, and glyph-wardens, often serving as specialized protectors for other races. Their strongholds are built into cliffsides and ancient forests, places with a strategic vertical advantage. Other races respect their prowess but find their social structures—based on dominance, physical skill, and complex honor codes—to be rigid and aloof.

Physical Traits

Felinnari possess soft fur, elongated canines, retractable claws, and slitted eyes. Their movements display a predator's superhuman grace and agility. A rudder-like tail aids their balance, and many keep their claws meticulously sharp as a point of pride.

Mechanical Integration

🧬 Animal Traits (ATS +2)
• Feline Senses: Advantage on Perception checks based on hearing or smell
• Claws: Natural weapons, 1d4 slashing damage
🛡️ Glyphic Affinity – Guardian Outline
You may inscribe a Guardian Outline on your glyphs without increasing the Animent cost. Your wards and protective spells persist longer and are harder to dispel.

Quimera & Heritage

Felinnari heritage adds +5% to Quimera Percentage (Q%). Hybrid offspring often retain the keen senses, retractable claws, and graceful build, though sometimes diluted.

Sample Names

Velys, Kithri, Solaris, Mirri, Talon, Ashke, Jara, Kovu.
